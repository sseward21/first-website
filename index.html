Phillips academy is an amazing place to be if it is the right place for you. There is no denying the fact that we have all the resources kids my age and older could ever need. Phillips provides us with everything we need and more and I am grateful for having the opportunity to study here. Phillips is also a place where you struggle. The environment is one of competition and has most people on edge. For some the workload is something that they can't handle, but for others it the way that phillips academy changes you, and sometimes in a way you don't want to be change. These struggles make it very difficult to go back home and be the person you used to be. Philips is a great place with lots of resources, and tons of struggle.

There is a family within that group. I believe all artist find comfort in their craft and every time I sing I feel comforted by a group of students who love music just as I do. I also sing on my own in talent show and competition. I will hopefully have a youtube account up by the end of the summer and hope to rise as a leader within the singing community.
